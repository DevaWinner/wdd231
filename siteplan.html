Site Plan - Recipe Finder

Site Name

Recipe Finder

This name was chosen because it's simple, easy to remember, and directly reflects the site's purpose: helping users find recipes easily.

Site Purpose

The website aims to provide users with an easy way to discover new recipes from around the world. By searching through meal categories or ingredients, users can find dishes they're interested in. A "random meal" feature will also be available for those looking for inspiration. Recipes will include ingredients, instructions, and images fetched from TheMealDB API.

Scenarios

  • "I have chicken and rice at home. What can I cook with these ingredients?"
  • "I want to try a new dessert recipe. What options are available?"

Color Scheme

  • Color 1: #34495e (Midnight Blue) - Used for headings and important text elements.
  • Color 2: #e67e22 (Carrot Orange) - Used for buttons and interactive elements.

Typography

  • Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Used for both headings and body text for a clean, modern look.
